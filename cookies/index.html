Cookie Policy

How SideCash.ai, a product of Santushti Infotech Private Limited, uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: 23 July 2026.

1. What cookies we use

We keep cookies to a minimum and use them for two things:

  • Essential: a secure, httpOnly session cookie that keeps you logged in. It is set when you log in and cannot be turned off without breaking login.
  • Analytics: our public website sidecash.ai uses Google Analytics (GA4), which sets cookies to count visitors and understand where they come from and which pages they view. This measures traffic in aggregate.

2. About Google Analytics

Google Analytics helps us understand how many people visit sidecash.ai and from which regions, so we can improve the site. It is provided by Google, which processes this data as our service provider. The reports are visible only to us, not to the public.

You can opt out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing Google's official browser add-on, or by blocking analytics cookies in your browser. Blocking it does not affect your ability to use the site or to sign up.

3. What we do not do

We do not use cookies to sell your data or build advertising profiles, and we do not embed third-party advertising trackers. Sensitive information such as identity or bank details is never stored in cookies.

4. Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. Blocking the essential session cookie will prevent you from staying logged in. Blocking analytics cookies is fine and will not stop you using the site.

5. Contact

Questions about cookies: support@sidecash.ai.
