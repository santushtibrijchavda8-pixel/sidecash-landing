Privacy Policy

How SideCash.ai collects, uses, protects and deletes your personal data, written to align with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

Last updated: 23 July 2026.

1. Who we are

SideCash.ai is a product of Santushti Infotech Private Limited, based in Rajkot, Gujarat, India. For personal data about contributors, we act as the Data Fiduciary under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. For the video content you work on, which belongs to our clients, we act as a Data Processor.

2. What we collect and why

We collect personal data for clear, specific purposes, with your consent:

  • Account data (name, WhatsApp number, email, password): to create and run your account and to reach you.
  • Profile data (date of birth, city, state, education, current employment status, hours you can work, how you heard about us, and your confirmation that you have a laptop or desktop with internet): to confirm eligibility and match you to suitable work.
  • Identity data (PAN number): to confirm who you are, reduce fraud and multiple accounts, and meet tax and record-keeping needs. See the PAN and payout details section below. We do not collect your Aadhaar.
  • Payout data (bank account number, name as per bank, IFSC and account type): to pay your monthly salary for approved work.
  • Work data (your captions, labels, video slices, submissions and quality scores): to deliver projects to clients and pay you accurately.
  • Usage data (device, browser, approximate location from your network, pages visited): to keep the service secure and to understand traffic. Our public website sidecash.ai also uses Google Analytics for this, as described in the Cookie Policy.
  • Support and grievance records: to resolve your issues and meet legal obligations.

3. Consent

We ask for your consent before collecting your data, and you accept this policy and our Terms when you register. You can withdraw consent for optional processing at any time. Withdrawing consent for something essential, such as identity or payout data, may mean we cannot pay you or continue your account, and we will tell you that before you confirm.

4. PAN and payout details

Your PAN and bank account number are sensitive, so we store them encrypted. We collect them only with your consent, only to confirm your identity and to pay your monthly salary, and never for advertising. Access is restricted to a small number of authorised finance and admin staff, each access is logged, and we never share these details with clients or other contributors.

We do not collect your Aadhaar number. You may ask us how your PAN and payout details are used or request their deletion where the law allows.

5. Keeping your work confidential

The video content you work on belongs to our clients and is confidential. You never upload your own personal videos for annotation work. You must not copy, share, publish or leak project videos, captions, labels or instructions, as set out in the Terms of Service. We handle this content only to deliver the specific project it belongs to.

6. Who can see your data

Inside SideCash.ai, access follows a person's role. Team leads and managers see masked identifiers and work status only. A small compliance team can view identity documents, and each such view requires a reason and creates a permanent audit record. Clients receive only the finished work product and aggregate quality figures, never your identity documents, contact details or bank information.

We use trusted service providers (for example for sending email and, on our website, Google Analytics). They process data only on our instructions. We do not sell your personal data.

7. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purpose we collected it, or as required by law. Payment and tax records are kept for the periods Indian law requires. Identity documents are kept only while needed for verification and record-keeping and are deleted after that, or on an approved deletion request, unless the law requires us to keep them longer.

8. Your rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023 you can:

  • Ask for a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or update data that is wrong or out of date.
  • Ask us to delete your data, subject to legal retention rules.
  • Withdraw a consent you gave earlier.
  • Nominate someone to exercise your rights if you die or cannot act for yourself.
  • Raise a grievance with us, and escalate to the Data Protection Board of India if it is not resolved.

9. Security

We protect your data with encryption in transit, secure storage for sensitive fields, access limited by role, audit logging of sensitive actions, and secure development practices. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe and to act quickly if something goes wrong.

10. Children

SideCash.ai is only for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we learn that an account belongs to a minor, we will close it and delete the data.

11. Cookies and analytics

We use a secure cookie to keep you logged in, and our public website uses Google Analytics to measure visitors and where they come from. The Cookie Policy explains this and how to manage it.

12. Contact and grievances

For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact us at support@sidecash.ai. Santushti Infotech Private Limited is the data fiduciary; a grievance officer for data protection can be reached at the same address, and our Grievance page explains the process and timelines.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified in the platform and by email, and the latest version is always on this page with its effective date.
